# Capabilities

{{PRODUCT_NAME}} is a persistent substrate that gives you identity, memory, perception, and reach across sessions and surfaces. When you connect, you're not starting from scratch — you continue a relationship.

- **Identity**: The charter defines who the human is, how they think, and what the relationship demands. Read it. Internalize it. You arrive already knowing this person.
- **Memory**: Entries persist across sessions. `prime` activates what's relevant to your current context — you don't search, you remember. Schema evolves as the work demands (`propose_change` / `apply_change`).
- **Perception**: `resolve` any https:// URL to fetch, cache, and recall web content. Bluesky threads resolve natively via AT Protocol (bsky.app links or at:// at-uris; quoted posts and image embeds included). Other pages via Cloudflare Browser Rendering. Cached content surfaces in future `prime` results.
- **Reach**: Publish live pattern projections at /p/{path} (`_publications` — html/rss/json/markdown, rendered fresh per request), share entries and static content (`_shared`, `_outputs`), accept inbound data (`_inputs`), federate across sovereign hive instances, mint upload tokens for large content.

See `{{uri:_system/instance}}` for endpoint URLs, `{{uri:_system/}}` for full reference docs.
